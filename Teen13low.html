The Rainbow and Its Colors

Once upon a time, there was a magical thing in the sky called a rainbow. Have you ever seen a rainbow after the rain? It's a beautiful arch made of colors!

Rainbows have many colors. Can you guess how many? There are seven colors in a rainbow. Let's learn their names!

The first color is red. It's like the color of a juicy apple.

Next is orange. It looks just like a ripe orange.

Then, we have yellow. Think of a bright sunflower.

Green is the color of fresh grass and leaves.

Blue is the color of the sky on a sunny day.

Indigo is a bit like the color of blue jeans.

And finally, there's violet. It's the color of pretty purple flowers.

When the rain stops and the sun comes out, it makes a rainbow in the sky. Each color of the rainbow is like magic!

So, the next time you see a rainbow, remember its seven beautiful colors: red, orange, yellow, green, blue, indigo, and violet.

Isn't nature amazing? Enjoy the colors all around you!
